Glossary of Key Terms

 * Financial Literacy: The ability to understand and effectively use various financial skills, including personal financial
   management, budgeting, investing, and debt management.
 * Podcast: A digital audio file made available on the internet for downloading to a computer or mobile device, typically
   available as a series.
 * Pop Quiz: A brief, often unannounced, test or quiz given to quickly assess someone's knowledge or understanding of recently
   discussed material.
 * Pedagogical Tool: An instrument or method used by an educator to enhance the learning experience and effectiveness of
   instruction.
 * Accessibility: The ability to be easily reached or obtained.
